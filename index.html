FAQs

Will other platforms be supported in the future?

Right now the current target is stable Windows releases only.


Building and deploying to other platforms, particular Mac/iOS, is quite prohibitive both in terms of cost and simply by the nature of this project.

Will multiplayer be added to the game?

A "proof of concept" for co-op multiplayer has been built and is promising, but it is still a while away from a full test release.


Should development of multiplayer continue smoothly, I'd like to explore other modes of multiplayer gameplay, both synchronous and asynchronous.

What content is planned for future releases?

Release cadence will generally follow the same original path of content releases, such a releasing story chapters in order.


As long as the content can be reconstructed from the original game files, then you can assume it will be included at some point.


Some content seems to have been stripped from later releases of the original game, e.g. some non-consequential story dialogue scenes
